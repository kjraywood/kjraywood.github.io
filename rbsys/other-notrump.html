10.2. Stayman with spades

This is not five-card Stayman because:
 • it includes 5 × ♠︎ + 4 × ♥︎
 • if a major-suit fit is not found, then   responder needs 3♠︎ as a relay to 3NT

To show five hearts, opener bids 3NT.

Following that, we swap hearts and diamonds to allow opener to declare.

We don’t need a way for opener to show five spades because responder has shown four.

The other Smolen-shape (5 × ♥︎ + 4 × ♠︎) is shown with a direct 3♦︎ (transfer to hearts with spades).
