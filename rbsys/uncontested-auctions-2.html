23. Two-over-one Auctions

 • 100% game-forcing  • 1M – 2♦︎ and 1♠︎ – 2♥︎ show 5+  • 1M – 2♣︎ could be short

23.1. Nebulous Two Clubs

Applies after 1♥︎ or 1♠︎ opening.

After a 1♥︎ opening, responder never bids 1♠︎ with GF values but only four spade-cards. Therefore, the nebulous 2♣︎ response to a 1♥︎ opening includes 4-3-3-3 and 4-3-4-2 shapes. After a 1♠︎ opening, a nebulous 2♣︎ includes 3-4-3-3 and 3-4-4-2 shapes. After both 1M openings, 2♣︎ includes 3-3-4-3.

Opener and responder use waiting bids below 2NT unless they have something to say such as extra length, extra strength, or a fit. The waiting bids could hide a very strong hand wishing to take control if partner shows any extras.

Following a nebulous 2♣︎ a jump by opener is NOT a splinter. A 3-level suit-bid below 3M shows a good two suiter; at least 5-5.
